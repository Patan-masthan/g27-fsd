Through today’s technology especially the Internet, I can easily find out about new movies. One of my favorite films would be The Fault in Our Stars. I have seen the film twice, and I am always in floods of tears by the end because it is so tragic. This film is based on the novel The Fault in Our Stars by the American writer John Green, which I must admit, haven’t actually read because it’s extremely long! The film is set in two different settings, which are Indianapolis and Amsterdam. It tells the story of a couple, who is fighting for their love at the same time fighting against their cancers. It’s a very long story with several sub-plots, which are too complicated to go into. The story opens as the journey of a sixteen-year-old cancer patient named Hazel Grace Lancaster, who needed a shoulder to cry on. She was then forced by her parents to attend a support group, where she subsequently meets and falls in love with the seventeen-year-old Augustus Waters, an ex-basketball player and amputee.
